The pianogrammer.

Tired of using your hands to copy code? Ever see a snippet of code that's so awesome you want to have it twice? Now you have the ultimate solution that allows you to utilize your feet in just like a pianist. Press one pedal to copy and another one to paste and you can have your code WET as you like.
